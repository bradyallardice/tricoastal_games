Sandmen, Inc.

Nightmares cured. Rest assured.

Chicago, 1956. A team of fringe psychologists have invented a new technology that enables them to enter their patients’ dreams and fight the Nightmares lurking there.

4 R.E.M. Cycles.
4 Nightmares.

Your team works through four cycles of sleep, facing one Nightmare in each. If anyone dies, everybody wakes up — and you don’t get paid.

Choose Your Condition

Nightmares set personal rules for each player. Win the most of a suit, win the led tricks, claim a reward, dodge a punishment. Meet them, or suffer the consequences.

Manipulate the Trick

The highest card wins the trick. But lower cards trigger the stronger actions. Every card you play is a wager between taking the trick and powering your move.

Make Your Move

Every card has a choice of 2 actions:

ConfrontDeal damage
DeflectBlock attacks
FocusGain Focus
ProjectUse a tool

Lead or follow suit and become the Nightmare’s target. Refuse, and trigger its Break Ability.
